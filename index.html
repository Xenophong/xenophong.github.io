about me

I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student at the Experimental Medicine Doctoral program of Munich Technical University, working on investigating gene-environment interactions (GxE) on complex traits, including mitochondrial genome, mito-nuclear interactions, psychiatric traits, and an extensive and diverse set of 48 tissues.

At the moment I am characterizing how genetic effects vary between tissues of the human body and trying to uncover the underlying biological mechanisms involved through causal inference statistical methods.
